About Lemay Flag Football League (LFFL)

Our Mission

At LFFL, we focus on building a community and fostering competition. Whether you have a full team or are signing up alone, we make it easy for everyone to get involved in competitive flag football.

League Structure

  • Separate leagues for Standard and VIP formats
  • Standard League: Monday, Wednesday, and weekends
  • VIP League: Friday and Saturday nights

Free Agent System

Players without a full team can sign up as free agents. Free agents are recruited based on their profiles, which include height, weight, position, and skills.

Bundle Option

You and up to two friends can sign up as a bundle and play together at a discounted rate.
